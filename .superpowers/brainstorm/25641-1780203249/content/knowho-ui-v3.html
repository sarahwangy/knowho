Knowho

Remember everyone you meet.

A simple web app that helps you remember the names, faces, and little details of every new person in your community — so the next hello feels like an old friend.

30 seconds. One friend. That's the whole idea.

The problem

Sometimes it's hard to remember a new person's name, or their habits, birthday, or the things you talked about.

And the moment you forget, the connection quietly slips away — the warm community you wanted starts to feel like a room full of strangers.

How Knowho helps

Three small habits.
Nobody forgotten.

1

记下来，30秒以内

刚认识一个人，打开 Knowho，名字 + 一句印象，搞定。地铁上也能完成。

2

见面前，先预热一下

翻开 Ta 的档案，看看上次聊了什么、生日快到了没，下次见面不再尬。

3

自动提醒，不费心

生日前3天收到提醒，发条消息，对方会觉得你真的在意 Ta。
