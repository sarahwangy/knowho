§ 04 — How It Works
Three moments.
One loop.
01
Log
After every coffee, call, or DM — add a quick note. Voice input means you can do it in 30 seconds on the go.
Claude Haiku auto-summarises and structures it into the contact's timeline.
02
Analyse
AI reads across your entire contact history — relationships, patterns, open threads, things to follow up on.
Relationship analysis surfaces trends you'd never notice from individual notes alone.
03
Surface
Before a meeting, open the prep card. Approaching a birthday, the calendar reminds you. Going cold — a nudge appears.
The right context, at the right moment, without having to dig for it.
